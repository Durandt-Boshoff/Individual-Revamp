About Netcafé

Exclusive to the Netcare Private Hospital Group

NetCafé supports the values and culture which Netcare wishes for their clients to experience when entering their hospitals. Not only should you feel like you are in safe hands in the hospital, but in NetCafé too.

Situated inside Netcare hospitals around the country, NetCafé is a peaceful oasis to step away from the stresses of the hospital. Designed to meet the needs of hospital visitors, staff and even patients. NetCafé offers an extensive menu to those in need of a recharge.

Takeaways delivered to your hospital bed

Not only do we provide a space for hospital visitors to destress, but we offer a service to in-hospital patients too. We offer Takeaway options to be delivered to bed-bound patients, as well as to the hard-working staff of the hospital.

A place of comfort

Netcare believed in creating a coffee shop that would offer a healing space within the walls of the hospital to re-energize. Like the hospital around it, it prides itself on exceptional, warm customer service and comforting, fulfilling food. The concept came about when the hospital recognized the need to address the emotional needs of visitors, patients and staff alike.
